# Shortcut Button:
  • D-PAD(Navigation)
  • SoftLeft(Select)
  • SoftRight(Start)
  • Enter(PAUSE/RESUME)
  • Button A(9)
  • Button B(#)
  • Load saved slots(Call)
  • Volume Up(*)
  • Volume Up(0)
# Save Slot:
  • Slot 1:
    - Press 1 to save
    - Press 2 to remove
  • Slot 2:
    - Press 4 to save
    - Press 5 to remove
  • Slot 3:
    - Press 7 to save
    - Press 8 to remove
